About The Agency

Our Story

Crafting Brands with Soul

Founded in 2015, BrandStudio began with a simple mission: to help Indian brands tell their stories on a global stage.

We believe that packaging is more than just a box; it's the first tangible handshake between a brand and its customer. From artisanal spices to high-tech electronics, we blend deep cultural insights with cutting-edge structural engineering.

Our team of 40+ designers, strategists, and engineers works tirelessly to create unboxing experiences that delight, sustain, and sell.
